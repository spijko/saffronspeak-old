Blue is a magical color - somehow calming and invigorating, both peaceful and cheery. Our bright blue and white Midnight Lotus is alive with this magic. I particularly love its use in a bedroom, because this is certainly a place that requires soothing notes along with bright ones. Remember that this print is available in both a single sheet bedspread, and a duvet cover, depending on how much weight and warmth you want on your bed. We'll take a look around the bedroom today, and using our hand printed Midnight Lotus as an anchor piece, create a serene space that you'll love to live in. Wall Colors

For our Midnight Lotus bedroom, I've highlighted three paint ideas from Lullaby Paints, maker of non-toxic, no VOC, odorless paints. Each color will take the room in a different direction, so play away with your unique vision! Royal Blue should probably be saved for an accent wall, but oh what a pop that would be! White accessories on that wall would be crisp, clean and bold. I'm drawn to pale green walls in this room, and Celadon is right on target. This color acts as an interesting neutral and allows you to play up the "zen" nature of our lotus print. Azure Sea moves you in the direction of a beachy, Mediterranean look and is so young and fresh.

Wall Decor

Now that your walls are singing with new color, let's dress them a bit! I've chosen a range of pieces that are playful, global and romantic. A trio of Pink Pagoda's Chinoiserie prints could be arranged as a faux headboard, and Z Gallerie's hanging mirrors would certainly be an eye catcher (and a light catcher) across from your window.
