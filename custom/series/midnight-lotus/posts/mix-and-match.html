We've collected quite a few beautiful blue looks with our exotic-fresh Midnight Lotus print. Say you've decided you'd like to use it in a space (bedroom, bath, kitchen, living room), and would like to see how it works with other hand printed Saffron Marigold linens. Ideally you'll purchase a few generously sized swatches to play around with, but first, you may like to take a look at this Mix and Match post which will highlight a few lovely combinations.

Midnight Lotus with Blues & Whites

This is probably the most intuitive look for Midnight Lotus. Personally, I just can't get away from blue, white and metallic for this print. (True, there's a hint of gold in both our Nizam's Pearls and Vanilla Glacé, but feel free to use gold or silver accessories with these patterns!) Sticking with a blue and white palette creates a really fresh, clean look that takes global and puts it in a clearly modern context. If you want to really push blue and white, add an accent of our Moonlit Taj - almost the visual reverse of Midnight Lotus, as it is a white ground with blue florals. Midnight Lotus with Turquoise & Yellow

For a fun and funky original look, bring the bright blue of Midnight Lotus along side a cheery yellow and a pop of turquoise. This look says "happy, unique and free-spirited". If that's you (or your daughter), then consider this combination! In this Mix and Match, each print plays an important role, working together to build the whole look. Dance O Peacock picks up on all three colors at this party: blue, turquoise and yellow. Jeweled Peacock, from our Dreams of India collection, provides a glowing foil to the richer blue of Midnight Lotus, and ties in perfectly to the aqua color in Morning Dew. Of course, Morning Dew brings in a cheery yellow ground and a hint of tangerine as well. [caption id="attachment_19004" align="aligncenter" width="600"] Clockwise from Top: Midnight Lotus; Dance O Peacock; Jeweled Peacock; Morning Dew; Annie Elliott Living Room via Apartment Therapy[/caption]

Midnight Lotus with Browns & Blues

The beautiful thing about blue is that it goes straight from fresh and modern to cool and natural, all with the swoosh of a swatch. :) Midnight Lotus along with browns creates a beachy, rich look that is at home in a master bedroom or a bohemian bath. To create this look, choose from one of our sumptuous chocolate browns ( Gilded Chocolate and For the Love of Chocolate) and add Ocean Breezes as a transitional piece. This combination is especially nice if you already have lots of dark wood, leather furniture or beige-neutral walls.
