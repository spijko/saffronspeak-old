Bring Midnight Lotus into your living room, and you'll surround yourself with cool elegance, vibrant pattern and the hint of royalty that comes with rich blue. Our touchstone pieces will be a panel or two of exotic scrolling Midnight Lotus curtains, and perhaps a few throw pillows or a euro sham. We'll build around these with silver, white and more blue to create a look that is fresh and exotic. [caption class="col"] Midnight Lotus for the Living Room[/caption]

Furniture

If you love to entertain, you know that lots of comfy seating is a must, as are empty side tables - free to hold snacks and drinks. Of course, even if you prefer a bit more solitude, a well proportioned chair, ottoman and table work just as well for quiet nights spent reading! I love each and every piece below. They are transitional pieces that move from classic to modern to global with ease. These are all savvy finds, too---just like the pieces in your Saffron Marigold collection!

Wall Art

Ample seating? Check. Well rounded lighting? Check. Now it's time to add eye-catching art to your walls. When considering wall art, aim for something one-of-a-kind or handmade or quite unique. These are, after all, pieces you put up specifically to look at, so you might as well choose something that keeps your interest, and catches that of your guests. (And of course, that complements your hand-printed Midnight Lotus pillows!) Whether a print or a photo from a working artist, or a mirror with a hand-hewn frame, hang pieces that say, "look closer".

Sensories

Finally! All your hard work is done and you can either have friends over or put your feet up on your ottoman. Light a fragrant candle or two to create an atmosphere that speaks to all your senses, and take in the beautiful, serene scene around you. Candlelight flickering across your Midnight Lotus curtains is almost like seeing moonlight scattered across a lotus pond at midnight. Enjoy!
