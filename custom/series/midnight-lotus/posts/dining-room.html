Tableware

Whether you choose a Midnight Lotus table runner, napkin set, round or rectangular tablecloth (all hand printed on 100% cotton), you'll set your table best with wonderful blue and white tableware. Use highly patterned pieces if you are setting the table with a runner, or with our napkins. Use fully white or bright blue pieces if you spread a tablecloth.

Centerpieces

Silver and pops of fuchsia or hot pink will fill out your table in a most winsome way. I love small votives at a long table---you can see over them, and an assortment look elegant in a long line or an organic cluster. Use a taller centerpiece like the pink peony one below at a round table. Perhaps you could arrange flowers or fruits in a small silver pedestal bowl like the one from Hayneedle - this will add just the right amount of charm to your gathering!

Lighting

Updating lighting can be a daunting task, but I've sourced three ideas for your Midnight Lotus dining room that will add global chic in a hot second! (Well, that's a slight exaggeration...). The dandelion inspired Maskros Pendant from Ikea is (as are all things Ikea) all about the assembly. Their downloadable instructions will walk even a novice through snapping it together, and into place. If you're not up for all that, consider a simple table lamp with a great Ikat shade in blue and white! And finally, if you don't even want to deal with electricity, add a few Moroccan Lanterns to your tablescape, light a candle, and consider it done.

Accessories

Why do I always love the accessories so much? They're like icing on a cake---a bit superflous, but so pretty! Mirrors are almost a must in a dining room. We want light, light, and more light and of course, mirrors invite all kinds of natural light to bounce around and settle to create ambiance. In this lovely light room, let's tease out the lotus theme of our table linen by adding a stacking set of lotus plates to a side table or credenza---these are lovely and useful, too! Under the table, add a statement rug. Custom Cool Rugs is now one of my favorite shops - I could spend a long time dreaming about the huge variety of interesting rugs they offer! If you don't love the one I've highlighted, check out their site! You're sure to find something you like.
