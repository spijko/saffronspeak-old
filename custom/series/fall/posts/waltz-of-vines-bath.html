We celebrated Labor Day at the lake recently. That tradition always creates the perfect moment of saying goodbye to summer and hello to fall. Today, our fall bath ideas attempt to create another perfect moment - the place where summer merges into autumn, the memories of the shore greet the crunching of leaves, and the outdoors come in to grace your home.

Shower Curtain

Our hand printed cotton Waltz of the Vines shower curtain is a perfect transition piece. The sunny yellows will keep summer in mind, while also nodding to the golden hues of fall. Accented with robin’s egg blue and a bronze-beige, the muted palette is beachy, soothing, and will inspire wonderful bath decor.

Bath Linens

Tie in to the accent colors of your Waltz of the Vines shower curtain with rich, luxurious bath linens. You could mix and match, or use a single tone. If you have neutral walls, I’d choose either the Sage or Mineral bath towel sets. If your walls are colorful, a nice neutral (Bamboo) will be most pleasing. All of these cotton bath towels are available at Bed, Bath & Beyond--one stop shopping!

Storage

Bathrooms are highly functional and usually small spaces. They require a good amount of storage, and that storage has to work hard, and add to the overall sense of the room. The storage ideas I’m highlighting today are coastal in their sensibility and of a muted palette--just like our Waltz of the Vines inspiration.
