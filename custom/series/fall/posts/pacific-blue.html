Our Pacific Blue tablecloth for fall captures the last glimmers of summer sparkling on deep blue water, with golden leaves shimmering in reflection. Use this soft cotton, traditionally hand-dyed tablecloth to inspire your rustic yet classic autumn dining room. You'll love it!

Table Linens

We offer a variety of table linens in Pacific Blue, from heirloom quality tablecloths, to rich table runners and nautical napkins. In the fall, use natural and organic accessories and textures to accent your linen. Think dark wood, natural fibers, creamy ceramic pieces, dried flowers and decorative pieces in russet, ochre and sage.

Tableware

Place settings and service ware on Pacific Blue should pick up the creamy white background (it's wonderful to have everyday dishes in white, anyhow!) or play off of the deep indigo by using complementary coppers, metallics and browns.  Since this print is delightfully rustic, use dinnerware that is a bit more organic in color and shape.

Centerpieces

Can we just say that centerpieces, like icing on a cake, can be the most delectable part? Pacific Blue is such a wonderful foil for fall flowers--the deep blue just buzzes along with coppery-orange flowers and silvery green foliage.

Candles and Lanterns

Finally, fill your dining space with atmospheric affect by using nautically inspired lanterns and long burning soy candles.   Choose subtle, layered fragrances, and your family and guests will naturally be drawn to the table for conversation and warmth.
