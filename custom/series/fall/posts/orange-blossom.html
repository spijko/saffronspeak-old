Table Linens

The colors you love will sing and sweet scents will stir in the Orange Blossom dining room. Tableware

Play with the colors of the season, in vivid, deep organic hues. Tableware should be be bright, yet natural, so go for handmade goods and chunky dishes with undulating edges.

Centerpieces

To keep the design of Orange Blossom front and center, use a centerpiece in a single complementary tone, such as the metallic artichoke pieces or green glass bottles. Candles and Lanterns

Complete the look in your bright fall dining room with the ambiance that only the flicker of a candle can bring. Hang lanterns from the ceiling, and scatter votives across the table. When night falls, your dinner party will continue to sparkle and glow.
