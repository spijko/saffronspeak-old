As the warm days of summer start to give way to the cooler days of Fall, it's time for a little bedroom makeover. Whites and brights are perfect for hot days, but nothing says "warm and cozy" louder than deep, rich, vibrant earthy colors. Colors that will pave the way right into the Christmas holidays and the New Year.

Duvet, Shams and Bedspread

And that brings us to Ruby Kilim. Patterned after Turkish kilim rugs, its rich, bold, invigorating palette of ruby red, deep cream and grey-black will introduce warmth, color, and pattern to your bedroom. Blah will turn into beautiful, ho-hum will turn into handsome!

Sheets

Sheets make a personal statement and with a print like this, you have so many options. Muted and elegant; bold and brazen; dark and dramatic - pick the sheets that suit our unique personality and style.

Pillows and quilts

Nothing makes a bed more inviting than pillows tossed on them and a gorgeous quilt by its foot. The more pillows, the cozier the look. The quilt warms up the bedroom and adds plush comfort.

Sensories and accessories

Introduce accessories that highlight the global, almost tribal feel of the print. Pierced metal pieces and sparkling jeweled items combined with rustic elements provide dimension and interest.
We hope you've enjoyed these ideas on creating an exotic, warm bedroom for Fall. The deep red, tan and gray palette is sure to tap into the gorgeous colors you see out the window. We have more ideas for you for Fall. Do check them out.
