Sometimes all it takes is a little walk into your backyard to reap the benefits of the fall season and be inspired by things you see that have an organic, natural feel to them. The season brings with it unusual textures and vibrant colors that can rival any design catalog.

Curtains and Valances

The oak brown and goldenrod tones of the Waltz of the Vines cotton print are a lovely backdrop for your nature inspired autumn decor, but (bonus!)--they work well in summer too--just accessorize differently to bring out the brighter tones. The living room is one of the first places I want to "cozy-up" in my house…  I want it to be an inviting, warm destination for family and friends.

Pillows and throws

Now of course, I wouldn't be nearly as excited for fall if I couldn't snuggle up in a soft, cozy throw surrounded by comfy pillows. (Don't you just want to take a nap on the couch now?)

Accessories

Tap into the beachy feel and bring in elements that echo nature. Distressed wood finished pieces, faux beach objects and a ocean inspired print--these are just some ideas.

Floral ideas

Use harvest related pieces to create a tactile autumn experience. Gather branches weighed down with russet leaves, or fallen oak leaves in their myriad fall colors and organic shapes along with pine cones and put them in vases. And all you crafty DIY-ers, check out the tutorial on a fall wreath.
