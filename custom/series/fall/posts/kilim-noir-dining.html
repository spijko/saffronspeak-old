If the fall season has you yearning to be outdoors, then there's no better way to be connected with the earth and its seasonal colors than al fresco dining. For table linens, consider Kilim Noir, a Turkish inspired print that will have your guests enthralled with its brilliantly dramatic colors, and have you scrambling through your recipe books to dish up food magnificent enough to complement this exotic print.

Tablecloths, Table Runner and Napkins

Transport your guests' taste buds to the mysterious Far East with Kilim Noir. If you are worried that the deep black is too dark, don't be. The lustrous gold motifs are a perfect complement to the season's colors and used as a backdrop for your dinner with coordinated accessories, will be spectacular.

Dinnerware

Use dinnerware that highlights the feel of the print. Earthy terracotta dishes, natural rattan chargers, brilliant copper bowls - all speak to the global, handcrafted nature of Kilim Noir.

Lighting

Create a soft, intimate setting with glowing lanterns and candles. DIY string lights would be a perfect fall project that can see you through many seasons. Hurricane lanterns are a great option. They add light, they glow beautifully and stay put in windy situations.

Food and floral ideas

Get creative and serve up dishes where your guests will discover new tastes and exotic flavors. Like everything else keep the meal simple but satisfying. Green chili, garlic, sumac, parsley and mint add the exotic touch to a humdrum pizza guaranteed to tickle the taste buds and the conversation. Follow it up with a green salad, some grilled veggies and top it off with the favorite Baklava. Use seasonal branches and flowers to create a centerpiece.
And if by now your guests are not blown away, raise a glass and say "Şerefe" (pronounced Sher-i-feh) to really impress them.
