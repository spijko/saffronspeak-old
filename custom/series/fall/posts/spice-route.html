Is it the end of summer, or the beginning of Fall? All of a sudden, overnight, the weather here turned fall-like. The glorious weather has given way to a cool, cloudy day with rain in the forecast. But I'm not complaining. I love reds and oranges, and this season gives me the perfect excuse to bring those favorite colors of mine indoors.

Bed Linens

While summer was all about paring down, using summery linens and cottons and lightening up your space, Fall is all about getting cozy and cuddly with cozy sheets, warm, fuzzy blankets and woolen throws. Let's start this series with the Saffron Marigold's consummate fall print - Spice Route, with colors like cinnamon, turmeric, cranberry and blue all evocative of the gorgeous hues you see out your window in Autumn.

Quilts, throws and blankets

Just as in clothing, dressing your bed in Fall is all about layering. Nights vary from cool to cold to downright chilly, and so you have to be prepared for all its various fickle moods. Be prepared with quilts and throws in different weights and textures.

Curtains and Accessories

Earthy oranges, reds and browns are usually the colors associated with autumn, but blues and greens are as important, not just to balance and harmonize the other colors, but to soothe and calm the eyes after all that riot of color. Add global color and tribal flair to your fall decor with accessories that have touches of these complimentary colors.

Sensory Delights

Surround yourself with objects that will heighten the senses and enhance the colors of Fall. Warm, spicy fragrances and fall foliage will do the trick.
For many, Fall is the favorite season of the year! Not just for the burst of color all around us, but it is the best time of the year for close comfortable gatherings and family get togethers, hot soups. warm breads, games by the fireside. It's the beginning of the holiday season, a slow countdown till the New Year!
