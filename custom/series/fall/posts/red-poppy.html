Experts say there are two times of the year that are best for planting poppy seeds. Spring and Fall. What serendipity! Because while you are planing the seeds for a spectacular bloom in spring, be inspired by this child of autumn to makeover your living room "now" for an equally spectacular look with our Red Poppy linens.

Curtains and Valances

Not a print to scream out, nor hide in obscurity, Red Poppy is soft and elegant, showing off its gentle quiet beauty in pretty colors of tan and honey amidst red blossoms. Hang the curtains in the living room and the kitchen, and let its beauty shine through into your space. Its simple elegant colors are incredibly versatile to decorate around.

Wall Decor

A very easy way of changing of a room or space is with wall decor. If the idea of giving your walls a coat of paint to go with your theme seems daunting, highlight the walls with an assortment of art, mirrors and photographs. 

Accessories

Identify your focal point and begin accessorizing. Use nature-inspired tones help create an authentic global look. Mix up textures and don't be afraid to combine smooth metallic or glass accessories with rustic, rough hewn finishes. 

Sensory touches

Engage all the senses. Things that sparkle and shimmer for the eyes, soft throws that feel heavenly to touch, and reed diffusers and incense that perfume the air. Create an all out sensory experience.
