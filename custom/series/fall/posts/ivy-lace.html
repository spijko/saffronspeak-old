Entertaining is always fun, but fall evening get togethers are the best! The coolness in the air is a perfect setting for our cool white print - as well as hot soups and warm breads, cheese fondues, spiced ciders, mulled wine and baked apple pies!

Tablecloths and Napkins

Today, we're entertaining with Saffron Marigold's Ivy Lace table linens.  With elegant scrolling ivy vines on an ivory ground, the napkins and tablecloth provide a perfect blank canvas for your fall decor! Use them with spicy reds, russets and golds to mirror the fall colors outside, or keep them understated and elegant with blacks, golds, browns and creams. The neutral palette offers endless Fall decor possibilities!

Dinnerware and Glassware

Let your print be your muse while deciding on dinnerware. Choose items that accentuate the print yet let the beauty of the linens shine through.

Lighting

Introduce warm tones in candle holders and hurricanes to serve as a perfect foil for the cool white linens. For the unexpected, add a touch of dramatic black.

Centerpieces

Fall's bounty is so gorgeous, not just in the flowers outside, but the plethora of fruits and vegetables harvested...bring them indoors as objects of beauty. Pomegranates and red pears, or dried flowers, gourds and branches will look stunning on a buffet or as a centerpiece.
