The richness of Ruby Kilim can quickly take a bath from blah to beautiful, and since we're at the turning of the seasons, there's no better time to make the switch! Not only do the colors--persimmon, ochre, chocolate--tease out the tones of fall, but because they are earthy and natural, they are beautiful year round. Let’s take a closer look at the Ruby Kilim shower curtain, and bath accessories that can bring this look to your home.

Shower Curtain

Hanging a shower curtain is absolutely the quickest way to make a big impact in your bath. Ours are fair trade, hand printed and quality 100% cotton. Immediately, your space is elevated out of the ordinary. The colors and the motif of Ruby Kilim are inspired by ancient Turkish kilim patterns, and the cloth looks as though it could have been brought home from a far away market in another land.

Bath Linens

Bath towels are a necessity, of course, but because of their colors, they are wonderful functional design elements. Here are a few suggestions for towels that will work with Ruby Kilim. If you plan to go out shopping for coordinating towels, I recommend purchasing a swatch of our fabric to bring with you. These are large and well priced, and will help you feel assured you’re getting the right color.

Storage

A good amount of storage is required in the bath, and since most of this is visible, it needs to tie into the design as well. For this bath, I chose pieces that have an organic sense (natural fibers, patina finish, earthy colors), and a global feel. Accessories like this are just plain fun to shop for. These could even be items that you collect as you travel!

Accessories and Sensories

Once your shower curtain is hung, it’s time to look for a few more accessories and sensories that will round out the global vibe in your space. Mirrors are mainstays of the bathroom, but an unusual mirror becomes a focal point. Choose a glimmery, gilded one for the Ruby Kilim bath. Now add in the sensory pieces we use for bathing, adorning, or just plain luxuriating. Natural oils grace the body, and fragrant soy candles are good for the soul.
This fall bath will resonate especially with those who love a luxurious, global feel.
