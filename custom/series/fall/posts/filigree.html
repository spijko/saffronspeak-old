There is something about crisp, moonlit nights that lend themselves to bold black and white decor. Find the dramatic side of fall with the timeless look of Filigree table linens, topped with the glamorous shades of evening.

Table Linens

Imagine our elegantly glamorous Filigree print as the foundation for your dramatic fall dining room.  We'll accent with black, white, and a hint of warm gold, to keep the overall look sophisticated and welcoming.  Keep in mind that while I'm dreaming of using these during the fall, this print will translate fabulously to any season, with a quick change of accents and tableware. What a deal!

Tableware

For the stark, yet natural look we’re creating, I suggest using black or white plates. Each of the sets I’ve highlighted add interest and softness by incorporating other tones to their base of black or white. Of course, layering plates with chargers is a wonderful way to achieve a variety of looks with just one set of plates! I always gravitate toward burnished gold as it adds a hint of shimmer without being garish or overdone.

Centerpieces

Give an elegant nod to fall with luminescent natural white pumpkins, carved into a vase or simply wrapped in a decorative ribbon. If you prefer a centerpiece that you can use and use again, consider a dramatic candelabra or sweet mercury glass pumpkins from Pottery Barn.  These will look great on the table, surrounded by shimmering votives.

Candles and Lanterns

Add ambiance to your dining room with a few flickering lanterns and hurricanes.  The warm candlelight immediately says, "Come in, warm up, enjoy". Use varied sized candles alongside your centerpiece, and incorporate them in decor along a smaller table or buffet.
