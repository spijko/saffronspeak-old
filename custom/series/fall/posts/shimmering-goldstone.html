Take all the deep orange/red hues of the season, and sprinkle them with gold dust and you will have this lovely print from our Dreams of India Collection--Shimmering Goldstone. If the allure and mystery of India appeals to you, if you dream of traveling to exotic far away places, if you find yourself drawn to global hand crafted decor, then this hand blocked print with gold paste overlay may very well be your fall print.

Curtains and Valances

Shimmering Goldstone curtain panels will add a zip to your living room and the lovely orange print will transition beautifully right into Thanksgiving. Orange is a hot hue that shares the same attention-grabbing effect that red does, but with less fierceness and intensity.

Pillows

Never underestimate the power of pillows and throws. In addition to being the perfect accessories, they make guests feel right at home in an instant.

Accessories

Add interesting textures while choosing accessories to bring interest and dimension to your space. Calming neutrals like cream, white and grey will downplay fiery orange, bold shades of coral and gold will enhance it.

Engage the Senses

Nothing makes a room resonate more with warmth than a soft, welcoming glow of light. Have a pot simmering on the stove that shrouds your home in the spicy scent of pumpkin, apple cider, vanilla and cloves. Finally use the bounty of the season to decorate and enchant.
