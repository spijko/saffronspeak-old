There are certain days that just call to be celebrated... an unusually balmy fall afternoon, the first crisply cool evenings of the season. When the weather's nice, it's all about being outdoors - and that means dining al fresco. I love the look of white tablecloths outside. They are a wonderful foil to the colors of nature.

Table Linens

Vanilla Glacé is perfect for outdoor dining. By candlelight, the gold edging will shine in a way that's almost magical. In soft afternoon sun, the bright whites will dazzle. Use this as the inspiration for your party, and the decor will fall perfectly into place.

Outdoor Seating

You'll want to choose sturdy seating, crafted of natural materials. I’m partial to wooden folding chairs because of their style and functionality. So perfect for those of you who like to entertain! Choose between a natural yet polished look or a more rustic look. Either will look great against our handprinted white tablecloth.

Centerpieces

Since you’re already surrounded by the best decor around (the wide, wide world!), go simple and natural with centerpieces. Assemble found objects (branches, fall fruits and flowers, nuts, gourds) and add candles and glass canisters for a look that glows.

Candles and Lanterns

If your gathering is in the evening, then lighting is something you have to think about. There are so many opportunities to really create a wonderful, atmospheric sense with candles and lantern… these just may make your party!
