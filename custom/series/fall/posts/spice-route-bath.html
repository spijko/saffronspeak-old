Combat the coolness of the Fall air by adding the warmth of fall color to your home by way of linens and accessories. Fall days can be cold and blustery, and to walk into a home resonating with warm color and cozy comfort is a treat you deserve. This is a time for Spice Route--a print in cozy shades of deep orange, yellow and gold and a touch of turquoise.

Shower Curtain

Our Spice Route shower curtain has cinnamon, turmeric and turquoise motifs on a deep red paprika ground. Yes, all the colors of an iconic New England fall in one gorgeous print!

Bath Linens

Add a stack of creamy towels in a coordinating palette for a complete cohesive look. Pick serene colors like ice blue and ivory to relax and soothe, warmer bolder colors to refresh and re-energize.

Storage

Make mundane accessories like laundry baskets and hooks things of beauty by picking natural pretty textures. Small tables can double up as extra space to set things on, or even seating--if you are lucky enough to have the space for it. Fill your space with natural organic materials and textures that echo what you see out your window.

Accessories and Sensories

Nothing will lift your mood more than glowing scented candles that permeate the bath with fragrance and heavenly bath salts to scrub away all the stress of your day. Pick simple branches of a tree and put them in pretty vases--the effect is instantly soothing.
