Blue Holiday Centerpiece Idea

This beautiful assemblage centerpiece inspiration is so simple that it can be put together with no stress and little investment. I think the most important elements are the blue and silver ornaments. Once you've found those, arrange in a blue, silver or white bowl and center on the table! If you'd like to be a bit more crafty, take a cue from Centsational Girl and paint a few medium sized sticks white, and tie several more ornaments to the smaller branches. Place in blue tinted bottles on a mantel or sideboard.

Frosted Ornament Sugar Cookies

Isn't it interesting that vintage ornaments seems to have a resemblance to the shapes and lines of Indian and Moroccan motifs? Ornament shaped cookie cutters will be necessary for these sugar cookies, unless you're a whiz at free-form cutting, and have the time. Decorating cookies like these is time consuming, so I recommend a pre-holiday decorating party. Such fun, and everyone can take home a few treats!
