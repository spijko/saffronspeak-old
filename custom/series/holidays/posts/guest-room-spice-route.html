Camel trails in the desert, clamoring spice markets, exotic bazaars and alleyways, the lingering scent of incense in the warm evening air--this is the romance and beauty of Spice Route. A floral pattern of cinnamon, turmeric and turquoise contained within bold, baroque, lozenge like motifs, the vibrant palette will infuse warmth and global style to your guest bedroom. The bold deep red curtains or valance adorning the windows, and the stunning exotic duvet will make the guest room resonate with warmth and color! Guest Room Suggestion of the Day

Nothing says welcome like a basket of treats. Your guests will appreciate having fresh fruits, crackers, cookies and bottled water available to them in their room. If they like coffee, you could even set up a small coffee station with a single or two-cup coffee maker, cups, and all the fixings.
