Tableware, Centerpieces & Recipes

With our glorious handprinted linens headlining your table, you'll be able to round out a holiday table with a few simple touches. Here, ideas and inspirations for adding a bit of gilt, a natural touch, and sensational sweetness to your celebration.
Saffron Marigold

Waltz of the Vines

A very mellow yellow background gives way to a medley of wandering vines and flowers in sage, gold and ochre. We'll use this palette to connect to understated, natural elements like hammered gold, citrus fruits, nuts and seeds. If you like your holiday table to be organic and a bit understated, this is the table for you!
Pottery Barn

Gold Leaf Charger

Pattern upon pattern, these hammered gold chargers will add dimension and interest to table without competing with other elements. Rather, each piece and pattern combines to create a look that is warm and festive.
Southern Living

Citrus and Pinecone Centerpiece

I adore this winter centerpiece. On a footed bowl or stand, center a hurricane or lantern and a candle. Surround it with pinecones, lemons, seed pods, and maybe even a few shiny gold ornaments. Add eucalyptus, holly or magnolia leaves for fragrant greenery.
Southern Living

Turtle Shortbread Cookies

If you've read our blog long, you'll know that I favor featuring "foodie" foods and exotic treats. In this case, though, good old Betty Crocker recipe caught my eye. And why not, when she's created such a delicious combination of turtles and shortbread!
