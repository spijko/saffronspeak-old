I occasionally like to do family gifts at the holidays. It's fun to think of something that everyone will enjoy and that everyone can enjoy together! If you are putting together gifts for a special couple or family with kids, we've got some unique ideas for you! Start with one of our Dreams of India Collection euro shams. (Or two, or three! :) ) These are bright, cheery and generously sized washable pillow covers which will instantly brighten a room as well as provide great floor seating! Add a fun game and yummy snack, and you've got the perfect gift package for any family!

What's not to love about our dazzling turquoise sham? It's bold, it's zesty, and a perfect launching pad for an intense game of Hearts or Gin. My only caution is that kids may fight over it.
