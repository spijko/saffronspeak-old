Tableware, Centerpieces & Recipes

With our glorious handprinted linens headlining your table, you'll be able to round out a holiday table with a few simple touches. Here, ideas and inspirations for adding a bit of gilt, a natural touch, and sensational sweetness to your celebration.
Saffron Marigold

Spice Route Napkin

The zig-zaggy stripes coursing across the bright cinnamon ground of Spice Route add a festive energy to your holiday table. Pair with a complementary Spice Route tablecloth or runner, or use as stand-alone style with neutral linens and a burlap runner. Lots of "wow!" factor either way.
Pottery Barn

Punched Metal Chargers

Love your year round dishes, but want to dress them up for the holidays? You'll find chargers to be some of the most hard working elements in your tableware collection. I love Pottery Barn's Punched Metal chargers along with Spice Route. They remind me of the detailing on Moroccan lanterns, and will instantly make even simple white plates and bowls exotic.
Seigal and Thurston Photography

Natural Holiday Table Decor

Spice Route is glamorous year round. That's a fact. To tease out the "merry" factor this holiday season, create a simple and jolly design for each place with a bit of greenery, twine and a single jingle bell.
Skinnytaste

Cranberry Bliss Bars

Anyone who has tasted a Cranberry Bliss Bar--from that coffee shop--is likely to have fallen in love. One of my favorite food bloggers, Gina Homolka of Skinnytaste, has created a fabulous and lightened up version just in time for seasonal baking. I guess that means I can have two?
