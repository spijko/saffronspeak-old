I think that guest rooms are fun to decorate. Often, these rooms remain empty canvases until guests are imminent, and inspiration strikes! (Or at least we hope it does...) Today, I'll assist your inspiration by showcasing our most sultry, dramatic print: Kilim Noir. This Moroccan black, gold and red pattern is royal, global, and altogether stunning. You'll love the curtains or a duvet in a room with gold or red toned walls, and your guests will love feeling like they've stepped into an exotic hideaway. Mix and match accessories from our other kilim inspired print Ruby Kilim and see how exotic the room will look!
